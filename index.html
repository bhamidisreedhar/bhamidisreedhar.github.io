About me

I am currently a visiting scientist in mathematics at the Harish Chandra Research Institute (HRI). I was a postdoctoral research associate at IBS Center for Geometry and Physics (IBS-CGP) from August 2021 to July 2022. Previously I was a postdoc at Korea Institute for Advanced Study (KIAS) under the mentorship of Prof. Bumsig Kim. I completed my Ph.D from the School of Mathematics, Tata Institute of Fundamental Research (TIFR) in May 2017, under the supervision of Prof. Amalendu Krishna.

Research

I am interested in problems related to algebraic geometry. The questions that I generally spend time thinking about are related to K-theory, Chow Groups and Matrix Factorization Categories.
